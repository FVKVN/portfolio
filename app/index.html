Services

Graphic / digital design

Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving through the use of typography, space, image and color.

The field spans different media and common uses include: identity (logos and branding), publications (magazines, newspapers and books), print advertisements, posters, billboards, website graphics and elements, signs and product packaging. Digital design on the other hand is more focused on digital media, it's a broad term, much like graphic design, and it spans different sub fields like UX design, website design, etc.

I myself am a trained designer, I studied graphic and advertising design at Sint Lukas Brussels from which I graduated with a masters degree in 2011. My experience spans over different media from print to digital, so I'll be happy to take a look at your logo, website, birthcards, flyers, ... .

Front-end development

Front-end web development, or client-side webdevelopment is the producement of a codebase that enables users to see and interact with a specific website or application. It's a very fluid field: techniques, best practices, tools change almost daily. That said a typical website seen by the user usually consists out of three programming languages: HTML (which serves as structure), CSS (which covers styling) and javaScript (which enables the user to interact with certain elements).

My main aim is to deliver fast, responsive websites with the user's experience as main focal point. Besides this I have a strong interest in web animation and believe that small, on point interactions have a greater influence on the users overall perception of a website rather than one big eye-catcher. I believe that the best animations are subtle and hardly noticable, they also should work towards the goal of the website or app.
