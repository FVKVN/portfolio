About

Hi I'm Kevin and I am a front-end developer and graphic designer based in the lovely city of Ghent in Belgium. By day I work as a web developer at Kunstmaan, by night I'm Batman available for freelance work. So if you have a project that you want to get started or just want to say hi, feel free to contact me.

Services

Graphic / digital design

I am a trained designer, I studied graphic and advertising design at Sint Lukas Brussels from which I graduated with a masters degree in 2011. My experience spans over different media (from print to digital media), so I'll be happy to take a look at your logo, poster, website, birthcard, flyer,e-mail, [insert anything relevant that isn't mentioned already here].

Front-end development

My main aim is to deliver fast, responsive websites with the user's experience as main focal point. Besides this I have a strong interest in web animation and believe that small, on point interactions have a greater influence on the users overall perception of a website rather than one big eye-catcher. I believe that the best animations are subtle and hardly noticeable, they also should work towards the goal of the website or app in short: do not animate to animate but animate to enhance user experience.
